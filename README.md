# 🎧 AdSkipper

> Skip YouTube ads with a triple-tap on your earbuds. No touching required.

## The Vibe

You're vibing to your playlist. Then - **BAM** - an ad hits. You gotta tap your phone? Nah, that's too much work. Just triple-tap your earbuds and keep going. That's it. That's the app.

## Features

- 🔔 **Hears the "Skip Ad" button** - plays a ting when skip button appears
- 👆 **Triple-tap to skip** - use your earbuds, not your phone
- 🔄 **Works with YouTube** - detects skip buttons automatically
- 🚀 **Runs in background** - stays active even when phone is locked
- 🤫 **Quiet watching** - silently watches screen, doesn't bother you

## How It Works

```
1. App watches for "Skip Ad" button on screen
2. Button appears → you hear a ting 🔔
3. Triple-tap your earbuds → ad skipped ✅
4. Keep vibing without touching your phone
```

## Setup

1. Clone the repo
2. Open in Android Studio
3. Run on your phone
4. Enable Accessibility Service in Settings
5. Start Media Service from the app
6. Go to YouTube and wait for an ad

## Tech Stack

- Kotlin
- Android Accessibility Service
- MediaSession API
- Android Studio (obviously)
