## ✨ The Vibe

You're vibing to your playlist. Then - **BAM** - an ad hits. 

You gotta tap your phone? 🤚

Nah, that's too much work. Just triple-tap your earbuds and keep going. 🎧

**That's it. That's the app.**

---

## 🔥 Features

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| 🔔 **Ting Sound** | Plays a notification sound when "Skip Ad" button appears |
| 👆 **Earbud Skip** | Triple-tap your earbuds to skip - no phone touching |
| 📺 **YouTube Ready** | Automatically detects skip buttons |
| 🚀 **Background Run** | Works even when phone is locked |
| 🤫 **Stealth Mode** | Silently watches screen, doesn't disturb you |

---

## ⚡ How It Works

```diff
 1. App watches for "Skip Ad" button on screen
 2. Button appears → you hear a ting 🔔
 3. Triple-tap your earbuds → ad skipped ✅
 4. Keep vibing without touching your phone
```

---

## 🛠️ Setup

```bash
# Clone the repo
git clone https://github.com/ExploreInsight/AdSkipper.git

# Open in Android Studio
# Run on your phone

# On your phone:
# 1. Settings → Accessibility → Enable AdSkipper
# 2. Open app → Tap "Start Media Service"
# 3. Go to YouTube and vibe 🎵
```
